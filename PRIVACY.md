# Mosaico Privacy Policy

**Effective date: July 6, 2026**

Mosaico does not collect any data. Period.

- **No data collection** — Mosaico has no analytics, no tracking, no ads, no accounts, and collects no personal information of any kind.
- **No network access** — the app contains no networking code. Nothing you do in Mosaico ever leaves your device.
- **Your photos stay yours** — Mosaico uses Apple's out-of-process photo picker, so it can only see the single photo you explicitly choose. It never has access to your photo library. Saving writes a new photo using add-only permission; your original is never modified or read back.
- **On-device processing** — all image processing (mosaic effects and text detection) runs entirely on your iPhone using Apple's Core Image and Vision frameworks.

Because no data is collected, there is nothing to share, sell, retain, or delete.
